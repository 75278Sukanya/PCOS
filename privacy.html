Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters, especially when it comes to health-related information. This Privacy Policy explains what data PCOS Management collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

This app is a personal project. It is not a licensed medical service, and data practices described here reflect a small-scale, non-commercial application rather than a large healthcare provider.

1. Information We Collect

Type Examples
Account information First name, last name, email, password (stored securely hashed)
Health tracking data Cycle dates, symptoms, notes you choose to enter
Technical data Basic usage information needed for the app to function (e.g. login sessions)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To let you track and view your own PCOS-related data
  • To improve and maintain the app

We do not use your health data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your password is never stored in plain text; it is hashed before being saved. Your data is stored in a database connected to this application and is not shared publicly.

4. Who Can See Your Data

Only you can see your personal tracking data through your account. As the developer of this personal project, I may access the underlying database for maintenance or debugging purposes, but your data is not reviewed, shared, or used beyond what's needed to keep the app working.

5. Data Retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. If you would like your account and data deleted, you may request this, and it will be removed from the database.

6. Your Choices

  • You can update or correct your account information at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • You can choose not to enter sensitive details beyond what's required to use the app.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information (such as password hashing), but as with any application, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app develops. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please reach out through the contact information provided on the app.
